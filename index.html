About me

I've been developing iPhone apps since I was 14. I started out by creating small, simple games and gradually moved to full-stack development. My official career as a developer took off last year when I was hired by a Düsseldorf startup and have led the development of the company's first major iOS app. At the same time I developed Gluco (currently in the App Store), an app for people with diabetes. I currently live in Mülheim an der Ruhr, Germany. My current position is "iOS-Developer" for DiabetesLabs LLC, an american health-care-software company, where I work on DiabetesKit(iOS).
