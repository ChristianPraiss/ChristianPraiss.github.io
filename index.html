About myself

I've been developing iPhone apps since i was 14, starting out with small games and then moving to full-stack development during the last years. I am currently located in Mülheim an der Ruhr, Germany. My career as a dev took of last year, when I got hired by a startup company from Düsseldorf, for which I developed my first major iOS app. At the same time I started developing Gluco, the app for diabetics, implementing the technologies I had learned developing passpoints into it.
